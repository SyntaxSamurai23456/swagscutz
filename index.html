Our Story

Our founder recalls a bright green dress she adored as a child — green became a symbol. The DOT represents the moment she made a full-stop decision to pursue a greener, more conscious life. Travel through Europe's street cafés inspired Green Dot: street-level warmth, excellent coffee and mindful choices.

Our beans are a medium Brazil/Honduras blend — expect chocolatey and hazelnut notes. Recommended: Cortado or Flat White.
